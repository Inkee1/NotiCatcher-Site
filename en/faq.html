Subscription & Pricing

A keyword catch occurs when NotiCatcher detects a notification containing your configured keywords and triggers an alarm. Each successful detection counts as one catch.

These include crypto exchanges (Binance, Bybit, Coinbase, etc.), trading platforms (TradingView, Robinhood, etc.), wallet apps (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.), and Telegram. You can view the full list on the Pricing page.

Both Basic and Pro plans include a 3-day free trial. You won't be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be billed.

Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time. The remaining balance of your Basic plan will be prorated toward your Pro subscription.

The daily keyword catch limit resets at midnight (local device time) every day.

You can manage or cancel your subscription from the "My Info" page after logging in, or directly through Google Play Store's subscription management.

General

Yes. NotiCatcher can override silent mode and Do Not Disturb to ring loudly when a keyword match is detected. This is one of the core features available on all plans.

NotiCatcher is currently available on Android via Google Play Store. iOS support is planned for the future.

NotiCatcher processes notifications locally on your device. Your notification content is never sent to any external server. We only store essential account information for subscription management.
