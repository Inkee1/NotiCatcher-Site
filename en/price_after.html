FAQ

What is a “keyword catch”?
When a notification arrives and it contains any of your saved keywords (app name, title, or content), NotiCatcher triggers a loud alarm. Plans control how many of these catches you can make per day.
Which apps are “general apps”?
General apps are all apps except financial-related apps and Telegram. Financial apps are listed in the “Restricted apps” section above.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription from My Info.
